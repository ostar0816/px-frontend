Our Vision

Just because it’s called “crypto”-currency doesn’t mean it needs to be incomprehensible. Our team believes that the world needs a cleaner and simpler gateway into the world of blockchain.

Pyxis isn’t just the simplest and sleekest crypto wallet on the market. It’s the easiest entry point for tech-savvy individuals ready to explore the potential of the blockchain economy. Pyxis educates you and equips you to do so safely and responsibly.

Update 1: ChainHop®

Pyxis’ proprietary ChainHop® technology provides an unprecedented level of security and anonymity to user transactions by combining the unique properties of multiple different blockchains.

Update 3: SegWit Integration

Features of the Segregated Witness soft-fork improve the security and efficiency of Bitcoin transactions. SegWit allows for increases in onchain capacity and improves the scalability of the overall network.

Update 5: Dividend Room

As Bitcoin forks continue to occur and generate new powerful tools and applications, Pyxis developers are working hard to make each subsequent fork as natural an evolution for you as they are for the developers creating them. Simply put, when a fork occurs the new coins and their functionality come to you.

Update 2: Monero Wallet

Pyxis is excited to be rolling out wallet support for Monero, a leading cryptocurrency emphasizing security and privacy.

Update 4: Purchasing Bitcoin

While the initial release of Pyxis relies on established and trustworthy exchanges for the purchase of Bitcoin, integrating that capability into the Pyxis application will streamline our user experience and pave the way for more power features.
